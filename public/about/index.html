About Laravel Zap!

Why I created this

I always want a fast-loading site with as few (or no) external dependencies (I am looking at you 'production database') that could potentially bring a site down. I also wanted something that is simple to deploy, maintain, and manage, but I did not want to give up using Laravel. I looked at Jigsaw (which is really great by the way), but it was not exactly what I was looking for. MORE TO COME...

Where it is going

I think the application has potential for integration with a flat file database. I am researching that now. If I do that, I will might look at building an admin in order to help manage it. But I trying to keep this thing pretty simple. I also want to create a Vue boilerplate app with a Lumen backend. Just trying to find the time...
